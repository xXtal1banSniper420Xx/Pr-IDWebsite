Challenges

  • Lack of Awareness & Accessibility: 75.6% of youths rated their knowledge of DMP2025 as poor (1–2 out of 5). (ISEAS, 2025)
  • Other Priorities & Interests: 72.2% cited lack of time. 90% of students experience academic stress (NIE, 2022).
  • Perception That Opinions Don’t Matter: 48.6% feel their views aren’t considered. 58% say youth opinions are ignored in national decisions (TODAY Youth Survey, 2023).
